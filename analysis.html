For each parameter (reaction times, fleet composition, ...), this tool runs many headless battles where the parameter is varied on the green team while the red team is held at the baseline. The plots below show the average ships ahead at battle end (green alive − red alive) versus the parameter's value, with 95% confidence intervals from N trials per setting. The tornado plot at the top sorts parameters by the size of their effect.
