EVE Online Tools

Personal toolbox for thinking about Nightmare-fleet engagements: a real-time fleet battle simulator, and a post-battle damage analyzer that pulls per-pilot stats out of a warbeacon report.

Battle Simulator

Live 3D Nightmare/Scimitar fleet simulator. Tweak fleet composition, reaction times, damage modifiers and target-call behaviour, and watch two fleets fight it out in real time. Includes a sensitivity-analysis page for batch sweeps and a logi-optimization page that finds the Scimitar sweet spot for a given Nightmare count.

Damage Violin Analyzer

Paste a warbeacon battle report URL, pick a ship type and a side (by alliance), and get a per-alliance violin plot of damage done, with survivor / killed overlays and outlier highlights for the most and least productive pilots.
